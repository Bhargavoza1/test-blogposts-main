Mlflow is consists of multiple blocks with multiple functionality which help us to simplifying the ML workflow.
In simpler word, Mlflow will help us in various stage of our ML development and deployment.

Given below are key functionally offered by Mlflow

- MLflow Tracking
- MLflow Deployments for LLMs
- MLflow LLM Evaluate
- MLflow Projects
- MLflow Models
- Model Registry
